Build for humans

Identify which customers have the biggest impact on your product's success whether it's a B2C or a B2B product. Spoiler alert: probably not the ones you think.

Focus on value

Bring consistent, significant, and irreplaceable value to your customers and create a strong, long-lasting bond between them and your brand.

UX = Business

Create measurable user experience metrics and tie them with your business' KPIs, ensuring alignment between your customers' needs and your investors' incentives.

Methodology

1. Identify

Find out what actual benefit your users get or wish from your product or service.

2. Define

Set measurable KPI's which align with both your users' needs and your company's business model.

3. Assign

Choose (or hire) the best fit from your team to serve as inbound and outbound experience advocates.

4. Plan

Draft a long-term product architecture plan, followed by a definition of the very first iteration.

5. Coach

Join forces with key Product, UX and R&D team members to deliver meaningful and authentic experiences.

6. Loop back

Create a healthy feedback loop between your users and your customer success operation.
